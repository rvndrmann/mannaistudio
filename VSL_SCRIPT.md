# AI Director Hub — VSL Script (Video Sales Letter)

**Use for:** YouTube/Meta/Instagram ads, landing-page hero video, organic reels.
**Audience:** Aspiring creators (esp. India) who want to make money / build a career with AI video.
**Goal:** Free sign-ups → AI Director Hub Pro (₹299/month Early Member offer, locked forever).
**Tone:** Punchy, founder-to-creator, urgent but credible. Show real work, real workflow.
**Brand:** Lime + black. CTA destination: aidirectorhub.com

---

## FULL VSL (~75–90 sec)

### 1. HOOK (0:00–0:08)
> "What if you could make Hollywood-style videos… without a camera, a crew, or a film degree — using nothing but AI?"

*(B-roll: fast cuts of cinematic AI clips — the train chase, dramatic shots.)*

> "Everyone's talking about AI video. But most people are stuck — random tools, ugly clips, no real workflow. They mess around for weeks and still can't make anything that looks *professional.*"

### 3. THE TURN / SOLUTION (0:20–0:38)
> "AI Director Hub fixes that. It's a complete, step-by-step system to go from idea to finished AI film — scriptwriting with AI agents, prompt engineering for tools like Seedance and Kling, character and scene generation, and editing."
>
> "Our **AI Filmmaking Crash Course** walks you through the exact workflow we use to produce agency-level films."

*(Screen recording: the course page, lessons playing.)*

### 4. STACK THE VALUE (0:38–0:55)
> "But it's more than a course. Inside AI Director Hub you also get:
> – **Weekly challenges with real cash rewards** — practice and get paid.
> – An **AI Jobs marketplace** where you bid on real projects and earn from your skills.
> – A **creator portfolio** to showcase your work and land clients.
> And every new member starts **free** — with **20 free bids** to jump straight into jobs."

*(Quick montage: challenges page, AI jobs, portfolio, the lime UI.)*

### 5. THE OFFER + URGENCY (0:55–1:10)
> "Right now we're in **Early Member** launch pricing — just **₹299 a month, locked in forever.** When the countdown hits zero it goes back to ₹799. Lock your rate now and never pay more."

*(Show the ₹799 → ₹299 price with the live countdown clock.)*

### 6. CTA (1:10–1:20)
> "Become an AI Director. Start free, learn the full workflow, and turn your skills into income. Tap the link, sign in with Google, and you're in — in 10 seconds."
>
> **"AI Director Hub — your skills, on autopilot. Go to aidirectorhub.com now."**
