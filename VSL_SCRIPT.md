## PRODUCTION NOTES

- **Voiceover:** Energetic male/female VO, Indian English accent, 150–160 wpm. Generate with ElevenLabs or record naturally.
- **Music:** Driving cinematic electronic track, duck -8db under VO, swell at section transitions and final CTA.
- **Pace:** No shot longer than 3 seconds except screen recordings (max 5s).
- **Captions:** Hard subtitles throughout (most viewers watch muted).
- **Aspect ratios to export:** 16:9 (website/YouTube), 9:16 (Reels/Shorts ads), 1:1 (feed ads).
- **First 3 seconds rule:** The hook visual must be your single most impressive AI shot.
